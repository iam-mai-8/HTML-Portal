About Me

Hello! My name is Maira Yanez and I am First Gen Latina in a journey of becoming a Software Development Engineer in Test.

After being in the Clerical side of the Medical Field for more than five years, I decided to challenge myself and explore my unknown passion for technology.

I say unknown because after taking an IT Course as an elective; I discovered a whole new world!

That "New world" took me into diving deeper into the Development of Software specifically testing.

I can happily say that this curiosity brought me to Year Up a program where they help young adults enter the job force by providing education, coaching and experience.

With these skills and growth I desire to input my curiosity , dedication and adept persona into the world of Testing and Development.
